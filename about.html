📖 About Deeni Rehbar

Deeni Rehbar is an Islamic platform created with the sincere intention of helping people strengthen their relationship with Allah ﷻ. Our goal is to make authentic Islamic knowledge easy to access through a simple, beautiful and user-friendly experience. Whether you want to read the Quran, learn Duas, understand Namaz, use a Qibla Finder, read Hadith, or benefit from many other Islamic tools, Deeni Rehbar brings everything together in one place. This platform is designed to encourage Muslims to increase their knowledge, improve their worship, and remember Allah in their daily lives.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet meaningful: To help people strengthen their relationship with Allah ﷻ. We believe that every Muslim should have easy access to authentic Islamic knowledge, daily reminders, duas, Quran, Hadith, and useful Islamic tools that inspire them to live according to Islam. Deeni Rehbar has been created with the hope of becoming a source of benefit (Sadaqah Jariyah) for Muslims around the world.
