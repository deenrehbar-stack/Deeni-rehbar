🔒 Privacy Policy

Welcome to Deeni Rehbar. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our website collects, how it is used, and how we protect your privacy while using our services. By using Deeni Rehbar, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

📅 Effective Date

Effective Date: 27 June 2026 This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users of the Deeni Rehbar website and may be updated from time to time whenever necessary.

📖 Purpose of Deeni Rehbar

Deeni Rehbar has been created with the sincere intention of helping people strengthen their relationship with Allah ﷻ by providing Islamic knowledge, Quran, Hadith, Duas, Namaz guidance, Qibla direction, and many other useful Islamic tools. Our goal is to provide a safe, simple, and beneficial Islamic learning platform for everyone.

📍 Information We Collect

Deeni Rehbar is designed to respect your privacy. We do not ask users to create an account or provide personal information such as name, phone number, password, or address. The website only stores limited information that is necessary for providing certain features.

📌 Location Permission

Deeni Rehbar may request access to your device's location only for Islamic features such as:

• Finding the correct Qibla direction.
• Displaying accurate prayer times based on your location.

Your location is used only while providing these services and is not permanently stored on our servers.

💾 Local Storage

To improve your experience, Deeni Rehbar stores a small amount of information in your browser's Local Storage. This may include:

• Your selected language.
• Your earned coins and progress.
• Other small preferences that help personalize your experience.

This information remains only on your own device and is not sent to us.

📢 Google AdSense

In the future, Deeni Rehbar may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads that are more relevant to users. These advertisements are managed by Google, and users can learn more about Google's advertising policies through Google's official Privacy Policy.

📊 Google Analytics

Deeni Rehbar may use Google Analytics in the future to understand how visitors use the website. Analytics helps us improve the website by collecting anonymous information such as:

• Number of visitors.
• Popular pages.
• Device type.
• General usage statistics.

No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected through Analytics.

🍪 Cookies

Our website and third-party services such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics may use cookies to improve user experience and website performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer. Please note that some website features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

👶 Children's Privacy

Deeni Rehbar is intended for users of all ages, including children who wish to learn about Islam. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us through the Contact Us page so that appropriate action can be taken.

🔐 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to help keep the website secure and protect the limited information stored on your device. However, no method of data transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. While we work to provide a safe experience, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

🌐 Third-Party Services

Our website may use trusted third-party services such as Google AdSense, Google Analytics, Google Maps, and other Islamic APIs to provide certain features. These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them if they wish to learn more.

🔄 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time whenever new features, services, or legal requirements are introduced. Any updates will be published on this page with the latest effective date.

📞 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Us page.
